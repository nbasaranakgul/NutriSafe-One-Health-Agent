# NutriSafe-One-Health-Agent

NutriSafe One Health Agent is an AI-powered food product intelligence system that combines food science, nutrition analysis, bioinformatics-inspired microbial risk reasoning, data science, and One Health concepts.
The project allows users to enter food product information such as ingredients, nutrition facts, allergens, processing method, supplier country, and optional microbial testing data. The AI agent analyzes the product and generates a structured report covering nutrition quality, food safety concerns, microbial hazards, labeling and documentation issues, and One Health implications.
This project was developed as a capstone AI agent project to demonstrate how modern agent-based systems can solve practical real-world problems in food safety, public health, nutrition, and bioinformatics.

**Key Features**
-Ingredient and nutrition label analysis
-Added sugar, sodium, fiber, protein, and calorie risk scoring
-Food safety and allergen risk identification
-Microbial hazard reasoning for pathogens and spoilage organisms
-Bioinformatics-inspired interpretation of optional microbial inputs
-One Health summary connecting human, environmental, animal, and food-system risks
-Structured AI-generated report with corrective action recommendations
-Portfolio-ready demonstration of AI agents in scientific decision support

**Example Use Case**
A user enters information for a fruit bar or gummy product. The agent evaluates the ingredient list, nutrition facts, processing method, allergen information, supplier context, and microbial testing results. It then generates a risk report that explains nutrition concerns, food safety concerns, microbial risks, One Health implications, and recommended next steps.
